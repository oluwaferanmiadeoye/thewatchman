Who are we?

Our Vision

To faithfully proclaim Jesus to the world, utilizing every available means to preach the gospel and spread His word to all. Like a watchman standing on the heights, sounding the alarm to awaken the nations, we take up the mantle of Ezekiel 33:7—alerting the world to the truth and calling all to salvation.

Our Mission

To proclaim Jesus Christ to the world through Music, Movies, Messages, Conferences, Prayer, Books, and Crusades—focusing more on singles and married, thereby averting pre-marital sex, fornication, adultery, divorce, etc.

The Mandate

To preach the gospel, awaken the nations, and call all to salvation, fulfilling the mandate of Ezekiel 33:7.

NAKED (A three part series book)

Like a rushing wind that scatters leaves and exposes the soil beneath,

Memories and Penitence draws us into the secret lives of Kunle Roberts, Chinyere, Sade, and Ade.

Kunle Roberts, a seasoned counselor, tries to dissuade Sade from making a destructive decision—one eerily similar to a choice that once left him scarred with deep regret. Ade’s world is crumbling fast; a seemingly petty mistake now threatens to derail his future. Chinyere clings to a past she’s unwilling to release, and her indecision may lead her down a road she’s not ready for—a road to perdition.

Will Sade heed Kunle’s advice? Can Ade save himself from the consequences of his actions? What secrets lie in Kunle’s own past? And what about Chinyere—what is she really running from?

This is a story of hidden battles, forgiveness, personal growth, and the quiet strength of faith.
